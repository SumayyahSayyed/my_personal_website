Projects

MeanArena Chrome Extension

A productivity tool that allows users to look up the definitions of difficult words on any website. By double-clicking on any word, users can access the definition of that word. Users can access the definitions from Merriam-Webster, Google Dictionary, and Wordnik Dictionary. Users can save the words and their definitions. After refreshing the page, saved words will get underlined. On hovering over these words, the definitions can be accessed.
